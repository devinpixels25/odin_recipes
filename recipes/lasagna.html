Homemade lasagna

Lasagna, but Homemade

Using only a few things, you can make this fairly easy.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sauce
  • Any spices you want
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Prepare sauce
  3. Mix cheese and sauce
  4. Layer how you see fit and bake in an oven
